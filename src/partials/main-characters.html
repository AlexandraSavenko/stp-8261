Main Characters

  • Leo

    A curious and fast-thinking puzzle enthusiast aiming to become a Match Master

  • Mr.Suffle

    The mischievous character who occasionally mixes up objects, making puzzles trickier.

  • Chloe

    A friendly competitor who challenges players to beat her high scores.

  • Boots Bot

    A helpful assistant that provides power-ups and hints during difficult levels.

  • Proffesor Mindwel

    A quirky mentor who offers tips and strategies to solve the most challenging levels.
